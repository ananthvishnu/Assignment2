The Dodge Challenger Hellcat

The Dodge Challenger Hellcat is a high-performance version of the Dodge Challenger muscle car. It is powered by a supercharged 6.2-liter V8 engine that produces 717 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque. The car features a unique design with a distinctive front fascia and hood, as well as a high-performance suspension and braking system. Other features include a sport-tuned exhaust, 20-inch wheels, and a variety of interior and exterior appearance packages. The Hellcat is capable of accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.6 seconds and has a top speed of 199 mph.
